About Online Trivial Pursuit

This website provides an online alternative to the cards which come standard in Trivial Pursuit games.

Simply select which board you have, what timeframe you want the questions from, the difficulty level, and then you are ready to go. Follow normal Trivial Pursuit rules. When a card is needed, select what category from the list, and the question will be displayed. Cards are displayed in a flashcard format, and tapping the screen will display the answer. The game fetches trivia questions dynamically, with no answer being repeated twice.

This website was created by Samuel Davy, to make Trivial Pursuit more enjoyable by being able to customize cards.

Their are known issues with this website, which is currently Version 1. Stay tuned for updates in the future.
